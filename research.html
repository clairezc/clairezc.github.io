Research

  • In semantics, I work on the contrasts between typical XPs and wh-XPs when they interact with the Mandarin particle dou, when it functions as a quantifier/distributor and a scalar marker. I propose that the contrasts described in my paper is due to the contradiction between disjunction and the universal quantification requirement of dou.

  • In syntax, I argue for the universal DP-hypothesis by claiming that Mandarin projects a DP, even though it is a language without articles. I am also looking into the nature of verbal nouns in Korean.

  • In psycholinguistics, I worked with Ph.D. student Laine Stanahan in Snedeker Lab, helping check the experimental files of a previous study about processing scalar implicatures under working memory load for counterbalancing. I also assisted in designing and producing the stimuli for an ongoing study about scalar implicatures.

  • Other research projects I have worked on include exploring the role of L2 in L3 acquisition.
